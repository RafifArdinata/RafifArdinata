Gunung Rinjani

Mount Rinjani is an active volcano located on Lombok Island in West Nusa Tenggara, Indonesia. It stands at approximately 3,726 meters (12,224 feet) tall, making it the second-highest volcano in Indonesia after Mount Jaya Wijaya
